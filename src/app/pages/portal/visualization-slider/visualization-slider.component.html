What is in here?

Data from different sources mainly routine data from DHIS2.

Areas covered :-

General information: Here there are indicators showing facility distribution and health workers by cadre

RCH: Includes indicators for Antenatal care, Labour & Delivery and postnatal care

HIV/AIDS: The indicators in the group reflect the three 90s achievements

Eye health: In here there are indicators for new eye patients and general status of eye health

Malaria: This area shows malaria testing rate, malaria prevalence and status of preventive methods and commodities

Nutrition: Here there are indicators for nutrition status for children under 5 years

TB & LEPROSY: Contain indicators showing TB and LP notified cases and drug use status
