Welcome to the Tanzania national health observatory

The tanzania national health observatory enables you to browse the data coming from different sources including HMIS routine collected event and aggregate data, programs reports, survey reports e.t.c

What is in here

Observatory themes

This is where you can get data about different health themes in summary and you can drill down to more details in the respective theme. Some are listed below

  • 1. Health performance.
  • 2. Reproductive health.
  • 3. Nutrition.
  • 4. HIV and AIDS.
  • 5. TB and Leprosy.
Data statistics

This component provide interactive data analysis (Defining data (indicators, data elements etc), periods and organisation units, choosing data presentation (graphs, maps and table) at different layouts) and comprehensive description of indicators with different data download formats (excel, pdf, images etc) at easy.

Downloads

You can download scorecards for HMIS, RMNCAH, BUSKET FUNDS etc and data from programs reports, census and surveys
